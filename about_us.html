    Customized bath

    While you wait for your bathtub
    take a look at some fun facts!

    Dry skin?

    If you are afraid of getting dry skin, a bath with a nice bathoil
    is the perfect way to keep your skin from drying out

    A soothing effect

    Bathing warms the skin and relaxes the muscles underneath,
    which causes a soothing effect - making the body relax

    A calorieburner

    A 1 hour bath at 38 degrees, can burn up to almost 130 calories,
    which is almost the same as doing 40 situps
    or walking for half an hour

    Lighten your mood

    Skin experts have confirmed that endorphins are released
    while bathing, similar to that feeling when you're laying
    on the beach enjoying summer

    Good for your heart

    Your heart has to work harder, when taken a hot bath, because it tries
    to cool the body down, which is great for the body's general blood flow

    Clean and happy

    Bath will make you feel happier, the steam from the bath wil open
    your pores and wash away the day.
    Clean and happy - what more could you want?
